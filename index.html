Landscape Maintenance

Fleck's Landscaping's professional, uniformed crews are dispatched to your home, business or multi residential site weekly to maintain the beauty of your landscape.

They will:

  • Cut all turf areas
  • String trim where necessary
  • Edge along curbs, sidewalks, driveways, patios
  • Remove landscaping debris

Other services can be added weekly or requested on an as-needed basis including:

  • Spring or Fall clean up
  • Fertilization programs
  • Weed control programs
  • Watering programs (Commercial or Multi-residential only)
  • Seeding or sod
  • Mulching flowerbeds and trees
  • Shrub or tree trimming and removal
  • Seasonal container planting
